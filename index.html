Description

LIFETIME WARRANTY

This will fit all campervans between a height of 210cm and 250cm.

The Lunar Campervan Driveaway Awning is perfect for a bit of extra space for living and for storage.  It also has extra large windows. The Awning includes tinted sky lights, u-shaped zip on all the panels, which can be used as a veranda.

The panoramic shape is perfect for viewing the great outdoors with plenty room for tables and chairs and all the panels can be removed to create a totally open canopy for your van. The one point inflation makes the lunar extremely easy to erect.

.
