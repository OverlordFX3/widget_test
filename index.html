Product Description

A New Zealand classic, Grass-Fed Lamb combined with broccoli, green peas, tomato, almonds, rice, and more!

The combination of nutrient-dense green vegetables and almonds with high-quality protein provides a wide and plentiful contribution of micronutrients, essential for best performance, and sustained energy to carry you throughout the day!

A complete nutritionally packed, low-calorie meal, enhanced with mint and rosemary for a fresh and hearty flavour profile, makes this an essential everyday lunch or light meal!

Specifications

Ingredients: White Rice, Tomato [Includes Non-GMO Maize Starch 2.4%], Grass-Fed Lamb (17%) [Grass-Fed Lamb (92%), Pea Protein Isolate, Parsley, Basil, Pink Himalayan Salt, Rosemary Extract, Sunflower Oil], Splited Peas, Broccoli, Almonds, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Mint, Garlic, Pea Protein Isolate, Rosemary, Brown Rice Protein, Pink Himalayan Salt, Sweet Paprika, Black Pepper

Contains tree nuts. May contain traces of milk, soy, coconut and fish.

Instructions: 

Main meals require 175ml boiling water. Ready in 5 minutes.
Gross Weight: 140g

1. Open: Tear open meal.
2. Rehydrate: Add water to rehydrate, stir thoroughly and reseal.
3. Enjoy: Wait for rehydration, tear open the second seal to create a bowl and enjoy!
