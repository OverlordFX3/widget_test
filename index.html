  • How will my flowers or plant arrive?

    How will my flowers or plant arrive?

    Our blooms travel in style.

    Carefully packed in a sturdy box, secured & settled, they travel with their own supply of water and flower food included for their arrival in the new home.

    The package also contains care instructions and a free greeting card, which will reveal who is the generous and thoughtful person behind their blooming surprise. Should you wish to remain mysterious and anonymous, simply don't enter a message and we will not reveal the sender of the flowers.

    Our plant gifts are watered before starting their journey, and the delivery also contains a free greeting card and care instructions.

  • Can I specify a time for delivery?

    Can I specify a time for delivery?

    If you would like for your flowers to arrive at a specific time during the day, you will need to select the guaranteed timed delivery option on the product page, which has an extra delivery charge.

    If you send flowers with our standard free delivery, we cannot guarantee a specific delivery time.

    Flowers sent with our standard free delivery can arrive anytime between 8am and 8pm on your selected day, on very busy periods like Mother's Day or Valentine's Day this can be even later.

  • Do you have a phone number I can call?

    Do you have a phone number I can call?

    To keep everything as fast and convenient as possible, we offer customer service via e-mail/contact form and chat. You can contact us using your preferred method of these two by using the buttons you will find below.

    Our live agents’ available hours are as follows

    • Monday - Sunday: 7am - 9pm GMT

    Outside these hours you can use our contact form, and we will respond via e-mail as soon as our agents are back in the morning.

Next day delivery

  • Your flowers are arranged, hand-packed and delivered by next-day courier directly from our flower emporium.
  • Your flowers will be delivered between 8am and 8pm, but if we’re exceptionally busy it could be between 7am and 9pm.
  • If you want to ensure you reach the recipient at home, please select one of our timed delivery options. We cannot guarantee the exact time your flowers will be delivered when you choose our standard free delivery.
  • If we can’t reach the recipient at home, we will leave your flowers in a secure place or follow delivery instructions provided. We cannot guarantee that we will be able to follow delivery instructions on each occasion.
  • You will get an email & sms notification as soon as your flowers have dispatched our flower emporium, and also as soon as they have been delivered.
Letterbox delivery

  • Your flowers are hand-packed into our signature letterbox in bud form,to ensure a comfortable route to their destination. They are delivered by next-day courier directly from our flower emporium.
  • The same delivery times, delivery notifications and guaranteed delivery options as above apply also to letterbox flowers.

In the rare event that a substitution is necessary when buying a gift, products of similar style and quality will be used, and the value of that substitution will always be of the same or higher value.
